## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, **please do NOT create a public issue**.

### Reporting Process

1.  Navigate to the **Security** tab of this repository.
2.  Click **"Report a vulnerability"** to open a private advisory.
3.  Alternatively, email **amar.sharma.dev@gmail.com** (or your private contact).

### What to Include

*   Description of the vulnerability.
*   Steps to reproduce the issue.
*   Potential impact (Data loss, Service denial, etc.).
*   Proof of Concept (PoC) code if available.

We strive to acknowledge reports within **48 hours** and provide a fix timeline within **1 week**.
